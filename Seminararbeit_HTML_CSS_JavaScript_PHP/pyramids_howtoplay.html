Pyramids

How to play




There is a side view of coloured pyramid.

Objective: Look at the pyramid in the first row. In the second row there are three pyramids viewed from above. Choose the pyramid which matches the colours of the first pyramid in the first column.
In order to make a choice, click on the button 1, 2 or 3.

Example:



Click "Go" to get the first image. The sequence of colours match the colours of the third pyramid. So choose the button 3.

In the end the buttons will be grayed out except for the "Submit" button. Click "Submit" to save your status.


You can save up to three games in total.
