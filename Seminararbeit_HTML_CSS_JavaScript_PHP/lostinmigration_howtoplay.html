Lost In Migration

How to play




There are black arrows (like a swarm of birds) pointing to different directions.

Objective: Focus on the arrow in the middle and choose the button of the same direction.

Example:



Click "Go" to get the first image. Focus on the arrow in the middle and choose the button of the same direction. In this example it is up.

In the end the buttons will be grayed out except for the "Submit" button. Click "Submit" to save your status.


You can save up to three games in total.
